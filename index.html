About Pokemon Unite

Pokemon Unite is my favourite online free multiplayer game. I play it on my Nintendo Switch. It is a battle arena game, unlike the other Pokemon games where you have to catch pokemon and battle others. In this game, you play as the pokemon and you battle to collect aeos energy and score on the opposing team's goal post. The team with the most scores win.
